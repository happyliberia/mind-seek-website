About Us

We are a youth-led organization that aims to provide the youth with introspective tools that cater to the well-being of the mind as they are empowered to be agents of change in their communities.


Mission

Our mission is to enlighten, empower, and empathize with the youth by providing introspective tools, resources, and spaces that cater to a healthy state of mind.


Vision

To see the Filipino youth pursue meaningful change in their communities as a result of healthy self-awareness, self-compassion, and mindfulness.



Our Values

We are an organization guided by Christian morals and values. Our hope is to see the youth freed from a spirit of fear, to one of power, love, and a sound mind.
(See 2 Timothy 1:7)


Our Initiatives


  • Our Community
    Join our Seekers community to connect with other fellow youth mental health advocates! For more information on this, click here to know more.
  • Webinars and Seminars
    We desire to enlighten the youth through introspective tools that cater to a healthy mind. We do this by partnering with mental health professionals in creating educational seminars and webinars to promote healthier coping mechanisms amongst the youth.
  • Q&A Livestreams
    Learn from the professionals themselves by tuning in to our monthly FB live Q&A sessions! Ask any question about mental health, psychology, and etc, and gain access to invaluable information from our esteemed guests!
  • Volunteering
    We as Mind & Seek firmly believe that youth empowerment and emotional healing not only involves looking inward, but outward. We aim to empower the youth to reach out to their own communities as agents of change. For more information about our volunteering programs, click here.
