Frequently Asked Questions


  • Who can become a Seeker?
    Any highschooler passionate about our mission and vision is welcome to become a Seeker! To join, check out our Join the Community tab to learn more.
  • How can I volunteer?
    To be aware of volunteering opportunities, sign up to be a Seeker today and stay tuned through our socials and FB group. If you have any questions, feel free to contact Timothy de Belen, our HR director, on Messenger.
  • What are your upcoming initiatives, events, and campaigns?
    Glad you asked! To be more updated about our upcoming initiatives, check out our socials on Facebook and Instagram through our Contact Us page.
  • Does Mind & Seek provide mental health services?
    Unfortunately, because we are a student-organization and are not licensed professionals, Mind & Seek does not provide formal mental health services such as talk therapy, medication, diagnosis, and etc. However, we do educate the youth by promoting healthy coping mechanisms, seminars, and connecting them with professionals who can better address their issues. For more information about this, feel free to send us an email at mindxseek.org@gmail.com
